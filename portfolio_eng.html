Flexsame

My internship at Flexso Digital took place from 17 February '20 till 22 May '20. My assignment during this internship was to develop a wallet app. An app that should replace the many RFID/NFC access cards that almost every consultant owns. In this way overcrowded wallets and an administrative hassle were avoided. I mainly chose to develop the app for native Android because Android offers more freedom in terms of NFC functionalities. I also had some experience in an Android environment, so the choice was quickly made. So I started working with the HCE features of Android.

Host Card Emulation is a method that was introduced with the release of Android 4.4. It allows your device to emulate a smart card without SE (Secure Element), a piece of hardware in smart cards usually found with financial transaction cards. It secures transactions to and from the card.

Poortsimulatie

Besides developing an app, I was also expected to create a kind of IoT gate simulation device that can communicate with a smartphone or tablet, using NFC technology. For this I chose to use a Raspberry Pi 4B with the NFC module PN532.
